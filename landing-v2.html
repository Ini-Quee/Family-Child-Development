The Solution
It's a Home Operating System

Most apps track tasks. That's a digital checklist — and it fails because it ignores how children actually develop motivation and self-discipline.

FamilyOS replaces enforcement with a living economy. Kids bid for premium jobs, build reputation scores, manage wallets, and pay a small family tax. They learn real-world skills — negotiation, scarcity, saving, and the value of consistent effort.

Meanwhile, parents get an AI-powered dashboard that shows not just what was done, but who your child is becoming.

How It Works
Five Steps to a Thriving Home
Step 1
Build Your Family Profile
Answer a few questions about each child — age, learning style, personality, goals. The system adapts the economy for every kid individually.
Step 2
Post Jobs to the Marketplace
Chores as standard tasks (fixed pay) or premium bounties (limited slots, bidding). Study sessions, reading goals, and sports practice count as paid work too.
Step 3
Kids Bid, Earn, and Grow
Children place bids, complete tasks, and submit evidence via live camera. They earn coins, build reputation, and unlock achievements — all on their own terms.
Step 4
AI Coach Guides Everyone
Your child gets personalised encouragement and gentle nudges. You get insights like: "Emma's grades improved after she started studying before dinner."
Step 5
Monthly Family Town Hall
Gather for a celebration — see total earnings, tax contributions, grade improvements, and the "Most Improved" award. Use the tax pool for a family experience.

Trust & Safety
Built for Families, Fortified for Privacy
Child-to-Child Firewall
Siblings cannot see each other's wallets, grades, or conversations. Each child has their own private space.
COPPA & GDPR-K
Verifiable parental consent. Data minimisation. Right to deletion. Children's data is never sold.
Privacy Firewall
Parents see insights and trends — never raw coach conversations. Children need a space to be honest.
Biometric Auth
PIN + biometrics. No sibling can impersonate another. Device fingerprinting prevents workarounds.
Evidence Integrity
Before/after photos are time-stamped and AI-checked. Fraud detection flags suspicious submissions.
Parent Controls
Bid visibility, leaderboard settings, economy parameters. Everything auditable, nothing hidden.
