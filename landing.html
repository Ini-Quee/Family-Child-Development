The Solution
It's Not a Chore App. It's Your Home's Operating System.

Most family apps just track tasks and send reminders. That's a digital to-do list, and it fails because it ignores how children actually develop motivation, responsibility, and self-discipline.

FamilyOS replaces nagging with a living economy — where kids bid for premium jobs, build reputation scores, manage wallets, and even pay a small family tax. They learn real-world skills like supply and demand, investing, and the value of consistent effort.

Meanwhile, parents get an AI-powered dashboard that shows not just what was done, but who your child is becoming.

Features
Six Pillars of the Family Economy
🪙⚖️
The Economy
Where Chores Become a Strategy Game
No more sticker charts that fade. Our marketplace lets kids bid for high-value tasks, compete with siblings, and manage their own wallets. They learn negotiation, scarcity, and the pride of a job well done — while you approve quality via before-and-after photo reviews.
📚📈
Academics
From Homework Battles to Study Bounties
Connect grades and study sessions to the economy. A-grades earn bonuses; struggling subjects trigger a compassionate AI study plan — not shame. Your child earns screen time or coins for consistent effort, making academics part of the game.
📱🔒
Screen Intelligence
Earned Screen Time, Not Argument Time
Set reading or homework blocks that gently lock recreational apps. The AI Coach encourages: "You're 15 minutes from earning an extra gaming pass — finish strong!" Screen time becomes a resource they manage, not a right you steal.
🧠💬
AI Coach
Every Child Gets a Personal Guide
Our AI speaks the language of growth: "You improved 12% this month. That grit is your superpower." It prompts reflection, celebrates streaks, and asks questions before judging. Kids talk to their coach — and you get insights, never their private thoughts.
🔐🏠
Security
Your Family's Data, Locked Down
Siblings can't see each other's wallets. The only person with full access is you, the parent. Child logins are biometric-protected, all evidence is time-stamped and fraud-checked, and the coach keeps sensitive chats private. GDPR-K compliant.
📊🎉
Family Vault
End the Month with a Family Town Hall
Automatic reports show earnings, tax contributions, grade trends, and the "Most Improved" child. In December, use the tax pool for a family experience — and see the kids beam when they realize they funded the pizza night themselves.
